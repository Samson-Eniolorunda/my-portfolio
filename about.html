About Me

Get to know the person behind the code

Hi, I'm Samson!

With a foundation in the humanities and a B.A. in History & International Studies, I bring a unique perspective to web development. My curiosity for how things work on the internet led me to pivot into technology. Through the Product Hub Africa Front‑End Bootcamp I mastered HTML, CSS, JavaScript and responsive design, blending creativity with code to build meaningful user experiences.

Today I specialise in crafting beautiful, accessible interfaces. I’ve delivered bespoke projects like a personal website for Nigerian footballer Lateef Kazeem and a real‑time digital clock web app, demonstrating both client‑focused portfolio work and technical tinkering. Each build deepens my appreciation for clean design, intuitive UX and maintainable code.

When I'm not coding you'll find me reading, gaming or exploring emerging technologies – hobbies that spark curiosity and fuel continuous learning. I thrive on challenges and am always eager to collaborate on new ideas.
